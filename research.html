Research at Jija Health Care during the COVID-19 Pandemic

Amidst the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, Jija Health Care is actively engaged in research to better understand the virus, develop effective treatments, and contribute to the global effort to combat the spread of the disease.

Our dedicated team of researchers is working tirelessly to explore innovative approaches and solutions to address the healthcare needs arising from the pandemic.

Learn more about our research areas:

  • Infectious Diseases Research
  • Medical Oncology Research
  • Clinical Haematology Research
  • Cardiology Clinical Trials

Research Areas

Infectious Diseases Research

Our research in infectious diseases focuses on understanding the transmission dynamics, developing vaccines, and finding effective treatments for infectious diseases.

Medical Oncology Research

We are conducting groundbreaking research in medical oncology to improve cancer diagnosis, treatment, and patient outcomes.

Clinical Haematology Research

Our research in clinical haematology aims to advance the understanding and treatment of blood disorders and hematologic malignancies.

Cardiology Clinical Trials

We are conducting clinical trials in cardiology to evaluate new therapies and interventions for cardiovascular diseases.
